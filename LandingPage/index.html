How does UniServe Pricing work?

UniServe offers a flexible, pay-as-you-go pricing model, allowing you to optimize your costs. You only pay for the services you consume, without any long-term contracts or hidden fees. How do you pay for UniServe?

Pay-As-You-Go

UniServe offers a flexible, pay-as-you-go pricing model. You only pay for the services you consume, without long-term contracts or hidden fees. This allows you to adapt to changing campus needs and optimize your budget.

By committing to a certain usage level, you can unlock significant savings on our suite of services. These plans provide predictable costs and ensure optimal performance, allowing you to focus on delivering exceptional student experiences.

Pay Less By Using More

UniServe offers volume-based discounts to reward increased usage. As your institution grows, you'll benefit from lower costs per service. Our tiered pricing model ensures that you pay less for higher usage. We provide variety of services to meet your unique needs.
